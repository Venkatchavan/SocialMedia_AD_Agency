## Hard Rules (FAIL = export blocked)
1. **No PII:** No names, handles, emails, or phone numbers in any output.
2. **No verbatim competitor copy:** Never reproduce competitor ad text word-for-word.
3. **No unsubstantiated medical claims:** No cure/treat/diagnose language.
4. **No financial guarantees:** No guaranteed returns or income claims.
5. **No cross-workspace leakage:** Client data stays in its workspace.

## Soft Rules (WARN = flag for review)
1. All insights must reference evidence asset_id(s).
2. SMP must be present and non-empty.
3. At least 3 RTBs.
4. Testing matrix must have at least 2 variants.
5. Uncertainties must include "UNKNOWN" rather than guesses.

## Platform-Specific
- **TikTok:** Follow Community Guidelines; no misleading health content.
- **Meta:** Comply with Ad Policies; no discriminatory targeting references.
- **X:** Follow DSA transparency requirements.
- **Pinterest:** Adhere to Advertising Standards; no misleading claims.
