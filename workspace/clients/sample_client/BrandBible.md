# Brand Bible — Sample Client

## Brand Summary
- **What we sell:** Premium wellness supplements
- **What we stand for:** Science-backed, transparent, effective
- **What we never do:** Make unsubstantiated health claims, use fear tactics

## Audience
- **Primary:** Health-conscious adults 25–45, active lifestyle, mid-to-high income
- **Secondary:** Fitness enthusiasts exploring supplement stacks
- **Awareness level:** Problem-aware, solution-seeking

## Voice & Tone
- **Adjectives:** Confident, approachable, evidence-based, warm
- **Use:** Active voice, short sentences, conversational but credible
- **Avoid:** Hyperbole, medical jargon, aggressive urgency tactics
- **Examples:**
  - ✅ "Backed by 12 clinical trials — see the difference in 30 days."
  - ❌ "This MIRACLE supplement will CURE you FAST!"

## Proof & Claims
- **Allowed:** Clinical study references, ingredient transparency, customer testimonials (anonymized)
- **Substantiation required:** Any efficacy claim must cite study or "results may vary"
- **Forbidden:** Cure/treat/diagnose language, before/after medical imagery

## Visual Style
- **Do:** Clean backgrounds, natural lighting, warm earth tones, product-in-context
- **Don't:** Over-saturated colours, cluttered layouts, stock-photo clichés
- **References:** Minimalist wellness brands (Ritual, AG1, Seed)

## Offers
- **Typical:** 20% first order, subscribe-and-save bundles, free trial packs
- **Constraints:** No misleading "free" language; always show terms

## Competitors
- **Main:** AG1, Ritual, Seed, Athletic Greens
- **Positioning difference:** Simpler formulas, transparent sourcing, lower price point
